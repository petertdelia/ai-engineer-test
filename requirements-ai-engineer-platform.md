a complete, AI-era engineering assessment that scores how well a candidate solves real problems with an AI assistant, and gives them a credible, shareable result.

The assessment
Realistic scenarios with supporting code, logs, and metrics — not algorithmic puzzles.
A built-in AI assistant that guides but won't hand over the final answer, so the candidate's own reasoning is what's measured.
Four scoring dimensions: Engineering Skill, AI Collaboration, AI Trust Calibration, and Engineering Judgement.
Scoring that measures the engineer, not the AI — rewarding effective AI use rather than penalising it.
Three modes: a short Trial, a Practice mode for rehearsal, and the timed Exam (the only mode that counts toward rank).
Difficulty control (Low/Medium/High), with more AI help and gentler grading on easier questions, and fairer question selection across technologies.
Integrity safeguards: timed sessions, inactivity timeout, leave-page warning, and flagging of likely outside-AI use for human review.
The question bank
~4,000 vetted scenarios across Software Engineering, Data Science, Data Engineering, and Cyber Security.
An automated generation and curation pipeline that produces, de-duplicates, quality-checks, and balances questions at scale.
The candidate result
A personal results dashboard with per-technology strengths and weaknesses.
A percentile rank, shown only once the candidate population is large enough to be credible.
A shareable certificate on strong results — as an image download and a LinkedIn share.
Learning follow-up: save topics to a personal list with real study links.
Accounts, brand, and trust
Real accounts via email and Google sign-in, with backend-protected data.
A public site — landing page, walkthrough, About, FAQ, and Insights articles.
A consistent visual identity with light/dark themes and full mobile support.
Privacy and compliance: cookie consent, data export and deletion, and a privacy policy.
